# Support

## Scope of Support

This repository is part of the VDA 231‑301 standardization activities and provides specifications, schemas, documentation, and discussion material.

Support in this context means:
- clarification of content and scope
- discussion of questions related to the schema and documentation
- guidance on contribution processes

This project does **not** provide individual or operational support.

---

## How to Get Support

### GitHub Issues and Discussions

The primary channel for questions, clarifications, and discussions is GitHub:

- Use **Issues** to raise questions, propose topics, or describe problems
- Use **Discussions** (where enabled) for general exchange and clarification

This ensures transparency, traceability, and equal access to information for all participants.

---

## VDA Governance and Working Groups

Technical and structural decisions regarding VDA 231‑301 are taken within the formal VDA governance framework.

Support and clarification may be achieved through:
- participation in the VDA Project Group (PG)
- contribution via dedicated working groups, such as the AP GitHub

Please note:
- these groups operate within the VDA framework
- participants and internal communication channels are not publicly accessible
- access follows VDA rules and processes

GitHub serves as the **public, technical interface** to these activities.

---

## What This Project Cannot Support

The following topics are out of scope for support:

- individual implementation or integration support
- consulting or project‑specific advice
- interpretation of legal or regulatory requirements
- access to internal VDA communication channels or participant lists

---

## General Guidance

If you are unsure how to proceed:
- start with a GitHub Issue describing your question or topic
- follow the organization‑wide Contributing Guidelines

Support is provided on a best‑effort basis within the scope of standardization and collaborative development.
